ANOTHER OTAKU SUICIDE

SHINJUKU, TOKYO
A young man, Toshiro Yoshiaki, age 22, committed suicide earlier yesterday. He jumped off the balcony of the 43rd floor of a converted hotel in Shinjuku, Tokyo. While he was still alive, he was known to isolate frequently from family and friends and spend days within the OASIS, often times without sleep or food. He passed away at 3:17PM, 7 February, 2048.

08.26.48 EDIT: As revealed by Parzival earlier today, he did not commit suicide, and was thrown out of the balcony of his apartment by IOI employees on the day of his death.
